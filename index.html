Channels

Color

  • Represents the attribute of hue
  • This is good mapping because the high contrast of the red and blue create visual pop out & is a color-blind safe combo
  • Also shows the grouping of results, with red and blue individually being one category, and mixed states showing both

Size

  • Represents the area attribute
  • This is mostly good mapping, because while the states are recognizable, the size of the states don't indicate the number of votes, and some of the states aren't proportional to the rest, such as Alsaka

Colormaps

Identity Channel

  • This identity channel is categorical
  • This is good mapping because the red and blue are very perceptually different for their own category with no overlap
  • They have the same saturation and value
